# Project Overview: CareBridge AI – HRSN Screening Navigator

## 📌 Context & Motivation

In modern healthcare, medical outcomes are heavily influenced by **Social Determinants of Health (SDoH)**. Factors such as unstable housing, food insecurity, lack of reliable transportation, utility service shutoffs, and unsafe home environments directly impact a patient's capacity to manage chronic diseases, adhere to medical treatments, and prevent avoidable emergency room visits.

Healthcare organizations use **Health-Related Social Needs (HRSN)** screening tools (such as the Accountable Health Communities (AHC) HRSN Screening Tool) to identify these needs. However, the current workflow in many clinics is plagued by critical inefficiencies:
1. **Paper-Based Inefficiency**: Patients fill out paper screening forms, which clinic staff must manually compile or scan.
2. **Delayed Interventions**: Referrals to social services are often deferred because clinic staff must wait for manual analysis.
3. **Manual Reporting**: Administrative reporting and tracking of clinic-wide social needs are done using fragmented Excel sheets, resulting in slow operations.

**CareBridge AI** was born out of a Healthcare Informatics capstone project that identified these friction points. It addresses the low screening completion rates and delayed referrals by migrating the workflow into a conversational, AI-driven assistant within **Slack**.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The core objective of CareBridge AI is to showcase how **conversational AI agents** can act as a catalyst for workflow efficiency inside care facilities:
- **Simplify Data Collection**: Guided conversational screenings within Slack enable clinic staff to enter patient feedback naturally.
- **Immediate Identification**: Clinical AI maps user responses to the 5 core HRSN domains instantly.
- **Automated Social Referrals**: Care teams (social workers, case managers, community partners) are alerted instantly through automated routing to specific Slack channels.
- **Operational Visibility**: Administrators gain real-time access to screening metrics via automated aggregate summary reports.

> [!NOTE]
> CareBridge AI is designed to demonstrate **process innovation and agentic orchestration** in care workflows. It is not intended to replace a certified Electronic Health Record (EHR) system, but rather to act as a smart collaboration layer.

## 🛠️ Key Social Needs Covered (The 5 Core HRSN Domains)

| Domain | Description of Need | Target Referral Team / Partner |
|---|---|---|
| **Housing Instability** | Homelessness, poor housing conditions, threat of eviction. | Case Management / Housing Coordinators |
| **Food Insecurity** | Insufficient access to healthy, affordable food. | Community Food Banks / Nutritionists |
| **Transportation Needs** | Inability to travel to medical appointments or collect medications. | Volunteer Driving Networks / Ride Share |
| **Utility Assistance** | Difficulty paying heating, water, or electric bills. | Local Utility Grant Officers |
| **Interpersonal Safety** | Domestic violence, unsafe living conditions, elder/child safety concerns. | Social Services / Safe Shelter Coordinators |
